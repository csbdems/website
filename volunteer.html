Get Involved

Contact us at ctdsbc@gmail.com to volunteer.

Help our Campaign

  • Knock on doors - We are out at least once a week talking to community members, help us reach more residents!
  • Support us on Facebook - Help people online know more about who we are and what we stand for.
  • Put up a yard sign - Starting 30 days before the election we will need yards for our signs, bring some civic life to your yard.

Contact us for information about canvas dates and locations and to get yard signs.

Help our Schools

  • Attend board meetings - Check the calendar. One of the best ways to support Cheltenham schools is to know what's going on. Board meetings happen on most Tuesday nights at the Administration building.
  • Support our schools on Facebook - There is a lot of misinformation out there. We need people to help keep passion around our kids grounded in fact, respect, and kindness.
